Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
Is this website free to use?
Yes, this website is free to use. You can access lessons, exercises, and quizzes without paying any subscription or hidden fees.
Do I need to create an account?
You don’t need an account to browse basic content, but creating one lets you track progress, save lessons, and access extra features.
How can I build my English vocabulary?
Practice daily reading, learn new words, use flashcards, watch English videos, listen to podcasts, and regularly practice speaking and writing.
Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, we offer certificates for completed courses. After finishing all lessons and assessments, you can download your certificate from your account.
